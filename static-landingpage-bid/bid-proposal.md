## Strategy Notes

### Strengths to Emphasize
1. **Shared philosophy** - Love for static sites and creative freedom
2. **Modern approach** - Performance, Core Web Vitals, semantic markup
3. **Demo offer** - Risk reduction for client
4. **Recent work** - liefermax.com just finished
5. **Long-term mindset** - Matches client's desire for ongoing collaboration

### Against Competition
- **Against template users:** Emphasize custom, modern design freedom
- **Against CMS developers:** Highlight performance and flexibility advantages
- **Against mass bidders:** Demo offer + quality references

### Key Differentiators
- ✅ Genuine enthusiasm for static sites (matches client's values)
- ✅ Fresh, relevant references
- ✅ Demo offer reduces client risk
- ✅ Performance-focused approach
- ✅ Open to long-term collaboration

### What NOT to Mention
- ❌ Pricing details (discuss after acceptance)
- ❌ Technical jargon that doesn't add value
- ❌ Negative comments about other approaches

### Tone
- Enthusiastic but professional

- Solution-oriented
