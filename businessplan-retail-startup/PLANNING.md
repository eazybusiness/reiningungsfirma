# Business Plan Project - German Retail Startup

## Project Overview
This project creates a comprehensive business plan package for a German retail startup that meets banking standards (Sparkasse, KfW, IHK). The deliverables include:
1. A professional bid proposal in German
2. A demo business plan document (Word/PDF format) in German
3. An Excel financial model with German headings

## Architecture & Structure
```
businessplan-retail-startup/
├── PLANNING.md (this file)
├── TASK.md (task tracking)
├── bid-proposal/
│   └── angebot-businessplan.md (German bid proposal)
├── demo-businessplan/
│   ├── businessplan-dokument.md (main business plan content)
│   └── finanzmodell.xlsx (Excel financial model)
```

## Fictional Demo Business Concept
**Company Name:** GreenStyle - Nachhaltige Mode Online
**Sector:** E-Commerce Retail (Sustainable Fashion)
**Target Market:** German consumers aged 25-45, environmentally conscious
**Business Model:** Online retail platform for sustainable, fair-trade fashion
**USP:** Curated sustainable fashion with full supply chain transparency

## Key Requirements

### Banking Standards (German)
- Must follow IHK (Industrie- und Handelskammer) guidelines
- Compatible with KfW loan application templates
- Meets Sparkasse business plan evaluation criteria
- Professional formatting and logical structure

### Content Requirements
1. **Market Analysis (Marktanalyse)**
   - German retail market quantification
   - Competitor landscape
   - Target customer personas
   - Regulatory environment

2. **Marketing Strategy (Marketingstrategie)**
   - Positioning
   - Marketing channels
   - Launch timeline

3. **Financial Projections (Finanzplanung)** - 3 years minimum
   - Income statement (Gewinn- und Verlustrechnung)
   - Cash flow statement (Liquiditätsplanung)
   - Balance sheet (Bilanz)
   - Break-even analysis (Break-Even-Analyse)
   - Sensitivity analysis (Sensitivitätsanalyse)
   - Funding requirements (Kapitalbedarf)
   - Investment plan (Investitionsplan)
   - Sales and cost plan (Umsatz- und Kostenplanung)
   - Debt service capacity (Schuldendienstfähigkeit)

### Technical Specifications
- **Language:** German (all documents)
- **Formats:** 
  - Business plan: Markdown → convertible to Word/PDF
  - Financial model: Excel with formulas
- **Financial Model Requirements:**
  - All assumptions visible and editable
  - Numbers must tie out across all statements
  - German headings and terminology
  - Clear formula logic

## Style & Conventions
- Professional German business language
- Formal tone appropriate for banking institutions
- Clear section headings following German standards
- Data-driven with cited sources where applicable
- Conservative financial assumptions (startup context)

## Bid Proposal Key Points
- Clarify scope and deliverables
- Address responsibility boundaries (client data accuracy)
- Highlight experience in retail sector
- Explain IHK/KfW template compliance
- Set clear expectations for revisions
- Professional pricing structure

## Quality Standards
- All financial calculations must be accurate and traceable
- Business plan must pass typical bank checklists
- Professional formatting throughout
- Logical flow and coherent narrative
- Realistic assumptions for German retail startup environment
